About Us

Likhéza is where art and culture become one in the making of eternal masterpieces. We are an innovative creative art agency, producing original, sustainable, and meaningful products using tikiw, a traditional and versatile material deeply rooted in heritage. At Likhéza, we are not mere creators; we are storytellers. Each product speaks of the wonderful history and art behind tikiw. As used with indigenous crafts for years, this resilient and eco-friendly material is the window through which one can see the harmony of nature and human ingenuity. We are reviving its use to honor tradition while making a sustainable future.

Why Tikiw?

Tikiw offers unmatched versatility, combining durability with a lightweight texture that’s ideal for functional art and décor. Its organic elegance transforms everyday items into soulful creations, bringing warmth and authenticity to your spaces. Beyond aesthetics, tikiw supports sustainability by promoting renewable materials and preserving traditional crafting techniques.

Our Mission

At Likhéza, our mission is to craft timeless masterpieces that honor cultural heritage, empower artisans, and inspire sustainable living. Through the innovative use of tikiw, we preserve traditional craftsmanship while creating meaningful and eco-conscious products that celebrate the harmony between art, nature, and humanity.

Our Vision

To become a global beacon of cultural preservation and sustainable creativity. We aspire to connect the world through the beauty of art rooted in tradition, transforming spaces and lives with our soulful, handcrafted tikiw creations.

Our Objectives

Likhéza’s objectives are to preserve heritage by reviving traditional tikiw craftsmanship and celebrating its cultural significance, promote sustainability by using eco-friendly materials like tikiw for a greener future, and empower communities by providing local artisans with opportunities for growth and sustainable livelihoods. Additionally, we aim to innovate art and design by blending tradition with modern creativity to craft unique, functional masterpieces, while inspiring global connections by sharing tikiw-based art worldwide, fostering cultural exchange, and promoting mindful living.

Our Purpose

Likhéza is driven by a mission to preserve cultural heritage, empower local artisans, and inspire conscious living. Through our products, we aim to foster a deeper connection to art, nature, and community.
